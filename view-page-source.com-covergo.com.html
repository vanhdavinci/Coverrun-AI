Is CoverGo right for you?

Whether you are a large global or small insurer, CoverGo is the right choice if you are looking to:

Launch a new product, fast

You want to launch a new product you know the market will love, but it still takes you and your IT team 6+ months to be done. CoverGo’s patented no-code product builder enables you to build and launch (and sell) products in hours, without even involving the IT team. Stay ahead of the competition with CoverGo’s product and distribution modules.

Fully replace my core

If you need a full core insurance system replaced, either due to the unsustainability of your legacy system or because you need a greenfield system, CoverGo is your solution. An out-of the-box, end-to-end, modern, no-code, API-driven, SaaS core insurance system that does it all, and for all product lines, ensuring you run the entirety of your business efficiently and sustainably.

Make claims more efficient

Are your claim teams overwhelmed with paperwork and manual processes? Is fraud, waste and abuse still rampant? Are your customers (and staff alike) demanding more transparency and faster claims settling? CoverGo’s claims modules enable you to make all this digital: automated processes, lower turnaround times and errors, considerable cost savings and improved experience, all key to making your claims more profitable, and enjoyable.

Help frontline sales do better

Empower your sales team, agents, and brokers with the tools they need to close more deals and keep customers satisfied. CoverGo offers a fully configurable digital proposal and quoting management system, seamless quote-bind-issue and straight-through processing, and user-friendly white label portals for faster and transparent customer onboarding and servicing. These tools and modules accelerate sales cycles, retain sales staff, and drive revenue growth.

Reach new customers

Are you eager to reach new customers through innovative channels or target the millennial demographic but feel hindered by your current systems and high acquisition costs, we’ve got you covered. Our digital distribution modules and comprehensive set of open insurance APIs empower you to create bespoke products that resonate with your customers and market them through the digital channels they frequent. From embedded and parametric insurance to micro-insurance and emerging trends, CoverGo makes it all possible.

Set the stage for innovation

Embrace CoverGo as your modern backbone, paving the way for innovation and future readiness. The platform’s contemporary architecture, flexibility, and comprehensive features empower you to adapt swiftly to changing market dynamics. With CoverGo as your foundation, your business can finally foster an environment conducive to groundbreaking innovations, ensuring your relevance and competitiveness in the evolving landscape of the insurance industry.

Launch a digital insurance branch or line

Want to open a digital insurance branch or launch a totally new insurance business line and need a new, fast-to-implement, modern and/or lightweight system to do so? CoverGo is your off-the-shelf platform of choice, offering end-to-end features in a low-risk, low-cost manner, so you can make your new business idea or strategy a reality right away. CoverGo can run as a standalone or be integrated with your existing system.

Enhance the customer experience

Today’s customers expect their insurance interactions to be digital and seamless, simplifying their lives. With CoverGo, you get access to the tools, features, and integrations necessary to deliver your products and services through your customers’ preferred digital channels. Our modern UX and UI ensure that your users enjoy the best possible experience in today’s digital landscape.

Flexible deployment model

Insurers have complete choice to deploy the CoverGo platform depending on their specific needs and strategic objectives. Our global team of delivery experts ensures that every deployment is executed to the highest standard.

Wrapper on top of legacy

Adopt CoverGo as a digital layer on top of your existing system. You can integrate with any legacy or third party systems thanks to our open API-driven architecture.

Core replacement

Adopt CoverGo in full, replacing your existing core system and migrating your portfolio to CoverGo.

Greenfield

Adopt CoverGo as a greenfield platform to launch new products and digital-first initiatives without legacy constraints.
